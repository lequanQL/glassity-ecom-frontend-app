Warranty Policy by Glassity
In addition to offering a wide variety of eyewear designs, friendly staff, and reasonable prices, Glassity also provides a clear and long-term warranty policy to ensure customer satisfaction.
  • Free vision adjustment support within 30 days if your new lenses cause discomfort (such as dizziness, headaches, or blurred vision).
  • 50% discount support (up to 500,000 VND) on replacement frames if your glasses break within 120 days.
  • 100% replacement support if your frame cracks within 7 days of purchase.
  • Frames purchased from Glassity partner brands: 1-year warranty for manufacturing defects.
  • Free lifetime services including cleaning, nose pad replacement, and screw tightening.
  • Free eye examinations and vision checks.
Thank you for trusting and choosing Glassity.
