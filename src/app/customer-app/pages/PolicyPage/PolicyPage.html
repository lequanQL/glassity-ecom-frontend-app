Shipping and Product Inspection Policy
Shipping Policy
Shipping Coverage – Delivery Time:
  • Within Ho Chi Minh City (urban and suburban areas): Customers will receive their orders within 1–2 days.
  • Other provinces/cities nationwide: Orders from Glassity are shipped via partners such as Giaohangnhanh, Giaohangtietkiem, and Viettel Post, with an estimated delivery time of 2–4 days.
  • Note: Shipping fees will be specifically informed to customers by Glassity before delivery, depending on each case.
Nationwide Delivery:
  • Major cities and provinces: Delivery time is usually 1–3 days after Glassity ships the order.
  • Remote or rural areas: Delivery may take 5–7 days or longer depending on the logistics provider.
  • Delivery time may vary depending on the delivery address and shipping conditions. We will do our best to deliver your order as quickly as possible and will provide estimated delivery details when you place your order.
Responsibilities of the Seller and Logistics Providers:
  • The logistics service provider is responsible for providing proof of delivery (including photos and the recipient’s signature) to both the buyer and the seller upon request.
  • Note: If you have not received your order within 7 days, please contact Glassity hotline: 1900 xxxx for immediate assistance.
Shipping Fees:
  • Shipping costs are calculated based on the delivery address and product weight. Customers will receive detailed shipping fee information during the checkout process.
Product Inspection Policy
Product Condition:
  • All products are carefully inspected before delivery. We are committed to providing high-quality products that match their online descriptions at the time of delivery.
Inspection Process Upon Delivery:
  • Upon receiving your order, please check the product carefully before signing for it.
  • If there are any issues with product quality, please contact Glassity immediately via hotline: 1900 xxxx, so we can assist you with an exchange or return according to our policy.
3. Exchange/Return Policy
Exchange/Return Conditions: We accept exchanges or returns in the following cases:
  • The product does not match the description on our website.
  • The product is damaged or defective due to manufacturing issues.
Exchange/Return Requests:
  • To request an exchange or return, please contact us within 3 days from the date you successfully receive your order. Glassity will assist you in processing the exchange/return in accordance with our policy.
